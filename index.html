The Problem We Are Solving

We are working on the following challenge for healthcare and business professionals:

"How might we provide a sense of active supervision for patients recovering alone so that they feel confident and held accountable to their recovery timeline?"

Our service aims to bridge the gap between clinical oversight and solo recovery, giving patients the support and accountability they need to recover with confidence.

About the Prototype

What is HeyBoss?

HeyBoss is a web-based medication and recovery supervision platform designed to help individuals — and their caregivers — manage daily medication schedules during a recovery period. The app provides a clean, modern dashboard where users can schedule medications, receive in-browser reminders, and track their daily progress.

Prototype Features

What the Prototype Does

🔐

User Authentication

  • Register a new account with name, email, phone & password
  • Secure login powered by Supabase native Auth (industry-standard)
  • Sessions persist in the browser — stay logged in after refresh
📋

Medication Scheduling

  • Manually add medications via a clean form
  • Each entry includes: name, time (AM/PM), frequency, and instructions
  • Frequency options: Daily, Specific Date, or Recurring Days of week
  • Data saved to cloud database — persists across sessions and devices
📊

Schedule Dashboard

  • View all active medications in a clean table
  • Each row: Medication Name · When · Time · Instructions
  • Mark a medication as Done — removes it with a celebratory animation
🔔

In-Browser Reminders

  • Background check runs every 30 seconds against the schedule
  • When a medication is due: shows a notification card, plays audio, and sends a desktop push notification
  • Each reminder has a "Mark as Done" button to dismiss
⏰

Automated Cron Reminders (Server-Side)

  • Server-side cron runs daily at 6:00 AM via Vercel Cron
  • External cron service (cron-job.org) pings /api/cron/check-meds every minute
  • Reminders fire even when the browser tab is closed
🚪

Secure Logout

  • One-click logout clears the local session securely
